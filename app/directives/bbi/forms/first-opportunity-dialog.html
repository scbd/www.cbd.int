First Opportunity Registration

Opportunity records you create reference 2 other BBI records, a Contact and an Organization. While creating your opportunity, you will be asked to select an organization and contact. If they do not exist you will have to leave the opportunity form and create them.
Procedure for creating an opportunity:

  1. Create the requesting Organization (if it does not exist)
  2. Create the contact person (if it does not exist)
  3. Lastly, complete your opportunity.
