First Contact Record

Contact records reference an other BBI record, Organization. While creating your contact record, you will be asked to select an organization to make that reference. If the organization does not exist you will have to leave the contact form. Create the organization, then return to your saved draft contact record.
Procedure for creating a contact:

  1. Complete the name portion of the record
  2. Select the requesting Organization or create it (if it does not exist)
  3. Complete the contact portion of the record
  4. Revier the record
  5. Save as draft or request publishing
