Welcome to Kronos

Kronos the meeting registration database that was developed by SCBD and which is used for preregistering and accrediting participants for CBD meetings and workshops. It is also used for completing the registration process onsite and issuing relevant badges. This system also allows online registration, meaning that National Focal Points and recognized CBD partners/stakeholders are able to register their delegations online.
