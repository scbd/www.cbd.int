Requirements


Media participation at CBD meetings is subject to accreditation by the CBD Secretariat. Accreditation is strictly reserved for members of the press who represent a bona fide media organization. Accreditation will be granted upon meeting the requirements. Requirements differ based on type of media and the job titles/roles.
