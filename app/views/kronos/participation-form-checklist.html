Media Accreditation Request

Preparation checklist

A digital copy on official letterhead of a media organization signed by the Publisher/Assignment Editor, Editor-in-chief or Bureau Chief, specifying the name and functional title of each member of the attending party. Unsigned letters or e-mails will not be accepted.

A digital copy of press the card/work pass for each attending member of your party.

 

A digital copy of the passport/national ID card for each attending member of your party.

 

The contact information of the person designated to be your organizations main contact with the SCBD.

 

The contact information of the person designated to be in charge of your organization.

 

The address and phone number while at the meeting.
