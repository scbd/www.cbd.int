Dashboard

The Bio-Bridge Web Platform's dashboard handles the submission, editing and listing of your Bio-Bridge records. All Bio-Bridge records are clearing-house mechanism records.

There are 3 main functions of your dashboard.

  1. List all records of that type.
  2. Create a new record of that type.
  3. See how many records you have of that type.

All Bio-Bridge records are clearing-house mechanism Reference records and are public to the world. All Bio-Bridge/clearing-house mechanism Reference records have 3 states.

  1. Everyone can see the record. It is owned by the Convention, yet appears under 'my records' becasue you created it.
  2. Only you can see and is owned by you.; and
  3. CBD is approving for Publication.
