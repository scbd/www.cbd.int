About the BBI Web Platform

What is the BBI Web Platform?

The BBI web platform is a tool for facilitating TSC. The platform is an integral part of CBD clearing-house mechanism (CHM), and is aligned with the Web Strategy for the Convention and its Protocols. The CHM will remain the repository for any databases and case studies of relevance to the implementation of the Convention and its Protocols. The BBI web platform will link to these resources, providing a list of curated resources that are specifically relevant to TSC. The BBI web platform:

  • Allows countries and relevant organizations to submit requests for assistance and offers of assistance online
  • Facilitates access to a wide range of knowledge assets and curated resources
  • Supports communication and awareness raising efforts
  • Support online discussion forums for knowledge sharing and exchange
