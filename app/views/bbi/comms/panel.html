    IUCN World Conservation Congress (1-10 September 2016)

    International Workshop on Technical and Scientific Cooperation for the Conservation and Sustainable Use of Biological Diversity and the Bio-Bridge Initiative (5 September 2016)

  • This workshop will facilitate exchange of information, views and perspectives regarding technical and scientific cooperation (TSC) for the conservation and sustainable use of biological diversity, with a particular focus on the Bio-Bridge Initiative and emerging experiences and lessons learned by other relevant TSC mechanisms & programmes.
  • The workshop will consist of two main sessions:
    •
  • Introduction to the Bio-Bridge Initiative and its Future Work Plan, including an overview of the draft BBI Action Plan 2017-2020 and pilot projects.
  • Panel discussion on "Enhancing technical and scientific cooperation for the conservation and sustainable use of biodiversity: Emerging experiences and opportunities for collaboration". Panelists include Dr. Tom Brooks (Head, Science and Knowledge Team, IUCN), Ms. Eileen De Ravin (Manager, UNDP Biodiversity Global Programme), Dr. Thomas Koetz (Programme Officer, IPBES Secretariat), and Dr. Spencer Thomas (Chair, CTCN Advisory Board, former Chair CBD SBSTTA).
